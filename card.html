Polish Tatra Sheepdog


This is my best friend Shogun, he is a representative of the Polish Tatra Sheepdog. It is originated in Podhale, region of Polish Southern Mountains - Tatra. These dogs have independent nature and strong will, simply they are very stubborn. But this make them very territorial, loyal and protective to their family with whom their form strong bonds. It is a large dog the average height for males is 65 to 70 centimetres. Their signature is massive white fur.

  • Likes: Our son Janek, cats, and best friend Lucky
  • Dislikes: Bikes, motors, intruders
  • Favorite foods: Everything left on table, counter and thrown by Janek on the floor
  • Hobbies: Sleeping, walking, playing with Lucky outdoor, barking for everything

Shogun is a proud representative of the breed with all advantages. He loves our family, is devoted and loyal.
